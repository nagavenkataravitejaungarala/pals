Edith Cowan University (ECU)

Edith Cowan University (ECU) is a prominent institution of higher education and research located in Western Australia, with campuses in Perth, Bunbury, and Joondalup. Established in 1991, ECU is renowned for its commitment to fostering innovation, diversity, and teaching excellence and is ranked as one of the world’s best universities under 50 years old. The university has 8 schools from science, engineering and technology, business and Law to arts and humanities that offer various undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs.

School of Science

The School of Science at ECU has Australia’s largest cyber security research and education program and is one of the leading groups in the world. ECU leads the Australian government’s Academic Centres for Cybersecurity Excellence along with security research and innovation hubs, state-of-the-art operations centers for training and simulation, and strong industry and academic partnerships that provide a realistic and immersive environment for research and learning.

To learn more about ECU, visit http://www.ecu.edu.au/.

PECAN+ is a Cyber Security Capture the Flag (CTF) competition

PECAN+ is developed and managed by the School of Science at Edith Cowan University in Perth, Western Australia for Australian students. This year, PALS in collaboration with ECU have brought the PECAN+ CTF Competition to all Partner Institutions. The initiative aims to raise your practical skills and understanding of security, digital ecosystems and other emerging technology domains, and to boost Industry and Academic connections.

On Feb 15th, the Qualifier round of the CTF Competition witnessed a battle of brains between 107 student teams across various PALS Partner Institutions. For close to 2 hours, student teams competitively solved several cyber challenges covering Cryptography, Digital Forensics, OSINT, Steganography, Software Exploitation, Web Exploitation testing out their problem-solving, team building and cyber skills to secure their ranking on the leader board. Top 40 teams have progressed to the finals on March 6th, IITM.
