Educational Engagement between Edith Cowan University, Australia, and PALS Partner Institutions

January 31st, 2024 - Online

PALS has collaborated with Edith Cowin University, West Australia with a focus towards building capabilities and knowledge partnerships in Cybersecurity.

In January, a virtual discussion was facilitated between ECU professors and PALS partner institutions to explore mutual areas of collaboration in research and education.

70+ participants comprising Heads of International Engagement Cells, Principals and Deans from our partner institutions attended this program with many expressing interest in engaging with ECU.

PECAN + CTF Prelims

February 15th, 2024 - Online

An online CTF prelims with 110 student teams participating from our partner institutions. From the prelims, the top 40 teams have been selected for the CTF finals.

PECAN + CTF Finals

March 5th, 2024 - Physical

The culmination of the above programs is the PECAN + CTF Finals on March 5th at IIT Madras Research Park.
